Features

Everything Built In.
Nothing Extra to Buy.

Every tool a professional audio engineer would reach for — included, free, forever.

🔇

Noise Gate

Automatically silences your mic when you stop talking. Perfect for cutting out keyboard clicks, room noise, and background interference.

🎚️

10-Band Equalizer

Precision parametric EQ from 60Hz to 15kHz. Boost warmth, cut harshness, and dial in your perfect frequency response.

🎙️

AI Noise Suppression

Deep-learning model trained on millions of recordings removes background noise in real time while keeping your voice crystal clear.

⚡

Sub-2ms Latency

Our BlueCore™ engine processes your audio on a dedicated real-time thread, achieving imperceptible sub-2ms processing latency.

🔊

Compressor

Smooth out peaks and normalize your voice level automatically. Broadcast-ready dynamics with a single toggle.

✂️

De-Esser

Tame harsh "S" and "T" sibilance frequencies that cause listener fatigue, without affecting the natural tone of your voice.

📊

Live Spectrum Analyzer

See your audio in real time. The waveform and frequency visualizer responds to every nuance of your voice as you speak.

🖥️

Virtual Audio Routing

Send your processed mic signal to any app — OBS, Discord, Zoom, Teams — without any additional virtual cable software.

🎵

Pitch Correction

Real-time Auto-Tune for vocals and voiceovers. Subtle correction mode keeps your natural sound while removing pitch drift.
